about

Dr. Somchart Raocharernporn is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, Faculty of Dentistry, Mahidol University. With extensive expertise in virtual planning 3D with face scanning in orthognathic surgery, he specializes in both conventional techniques and the surgery-first approach. Dr. Raocharernporn is a recognized speaker at numerous national and international conferences, contributing significantly to the field of digital dentistry and surgical planning.
